# Product Requirement Document: REN Health Landing Page

## 1. Project Overview
REN Health is a wellness brand dedicated to modern women and mothers. We provide traditional herbal wisdom with modern convenience, allowing busy women to prioritize their health without compromise.

## 2. Target Audience
- New mothers looking for confinement care.
- Busy professional women seeking wellness through herbal teas.
- Women who value traditional health benefits but need convenient solutions.

## 3. Key Features
- **Hero Section**: Strong brand identity focusing on "Convenient Wellness for the Modern Woman."
- **Confinement Packages**: Clear presentation of 14, 28, and 44-day herbal packages.
- **Miscarriage Recovery Section**: Dedicated space for the 7-day specialized recovery package.
- **Herbal Tea Series**: Showcase of the four primary tea blends.
- **Value Proposition**: Highlighting convenience, quality, and female-centric care.
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-friendly for busy moms on the go.
- **Credentials & Partnerships**: Highlighting support for major confinement centres (Cozzi, LYC, Byond28, Gracie) and partners (itsHerbs, Motherhood.com.my, Kelabmama.com).
- **Awards**: Motherhood Choice Award for Best Postnatal Recovery Essential.
- **About Us**: Heartwarming brand story ("A Love Letter to Every Woman") emphasizing female wellness and the collaboration with TCM specialists from itsHerbs.com.
- **Contact Section**: Interactive form with WhatsApp integration (60124238768) for direct inquiries, including Confinement Centre support.

## 4. Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Warm, Organic, Refined, Trustworthy.
- **Color Palette**: Earthy tones (Sage Green, Cream, Warm Terracotta).
- **Typography**: Elegant serifs for headings, clean sans-serif for body text.

## 5. Content Structure
- **Navigation**: Home, Confinement, Teas, About Us.
- **Hero**: Emotional hook + CTA.
- **Partners Section**: Credentials and trusted ecosystem.
- **Confinement Section**: Detailed breakdown of packages.
- **Miscarriage Section**: Specialized 7-day recovery care.
- **Tea Collection**: Visual grid of herbal teas.
- **Testimonial Slider**: Heartfelt reviews from mothers with manual navigation.
- **About Us**: Heartwarming brand story.
- **Contact Section**: WhatsApp-integrated contact form.
- **Footer**: Contact info and social links.
